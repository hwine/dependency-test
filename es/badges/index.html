Apply for Badges

Collect the Webmaker Training badges to show that you've completed your training and are ready to help Webmaker spread Web literacy using participatory methods and with an open ethos. #TeachTheWeb

Explorer

Show that you can:

  • define what 'making' means to you
  • explore other people's definitions of 'learning through making'
  • reflect on 'Connected Learning'
Go

Builder

Show that you can:

  • make a 'lesson' that teaches web skills
  • create or remix an Open Educational Resource
  • explain ways online communities give and receive feedback
Go

Facilitator

Show that you can:

  • playtest a teaching kit
  • comment and give effective feedback on other people's work
  • iterate a 'lesson' plan based on feedback received from peers
Go

Connector

Show that you can:

  • explain what is meant by a 'networked community'
  • co-design resources with other community members
  • invite others to participate in an online community
Go
